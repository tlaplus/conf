### What is TLA+?

[TLA<sup>+</sup>](https://lamport.azurewebsites.net/tla/tla.html) is a formal language for specifying systems used in industry and academia to verify complex distributed and concurrent systems. Some examples of the successful use of TLA<sup>+</sup> in industry are documented [here](https://foundation.tlapl.us/industry/index.html). TLA<sup>+</sup> is governed by the [TLA<sup>+</sup> Foundation](https://foundation.tlapl.us/index.html).

### TLA+ Conference?

Annual TLA<sup>+</sup> Community Events are organized in order to bring together industrial and academic users of the TLA<sup>+</sup> specification language and its associated tools. Occasional TLA<sup>+</sup> Conferences are organized with a stronger focus on the use of TLA<sup>+</sup> in industry.


#### Code of Conduct

TLA<sup>+</sup> Community Events and Conferences agree with and follow the [Linux Foundation's Code of Conduct](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/open-source-summit-north-america/attend/code-of-conduct/)!
